Care and Guide to Pet Pigs

Minature pigs have skyrocketed in popularity as housepets in recent years due to their affectionate, curious selves. They may not be the first pet a person thinks about when purchasing or adopting a new pet but they have many cat and dog qualities. They have best of both sides!

First, they can be easily litter trained or potty trained to go outside such as a dog or cat. This makes cleanup easier than having them not trained. They are extremely intelligent
